Pool Rules

  1. 1
    Each golfer is assigned a price based on their odds to win the tournament. Build your team using a budget of $30.
    Double-check your math before submitting — budgets over $30 will be adjusted per the commissioner's discretion.
  2. 2
    A $30 entry fee is due at the time of submission.
    Pay via Venmo @Patrick-Lawenda or PayPal to Patrick.Lawenda@gmail.com.
  3. 3
    You may select a maximum of 10 golfers. Your team score is the sum of all prize money your golfers earn at Augusta.
    Missed cuts and withdrawals (WDs) count as $0.
  4. 4
    The only way to submit your picks is through the official entry form.
  5. 5
    Multiple entries are allowed. Each entry requires a separate $30 fee.
  6. 6
    Tell your friends — the more the merrier.
